Garden City University is an UGC approved University in Karnataka. It offers many proffessional courses and is a great campus!!
Garden City University is established under Karnataka State Act no. 47 of 2013. The Garden City University Act received the assent of the Governor of Karnataka on 26th March 2013 and was published in the Karnataka Gazette on 12th April 2013.
